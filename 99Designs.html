What is 99designs?

99designs is an online graphic design marketplace where clients post design requirements and designers submit their creative work. The platform operates mainly on a contest-based system but also offers direct one-to-one projects.

99designs is owned by Vista (formerly Vistaprint), which makes it a reliable and well-established platform in the design industry.

Contest-Based vs Direct Projects

The main difference between 99designs and other freelancing platforms is its contest-based system. Multiple designers submit ideas, and only one wins the prize.

Direct projects allow clients to hire designers directly, which is more stable and predictable income.
