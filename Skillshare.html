1. What is Skillshare?

Skillshare is an online learning community focused on creative arts, design, photography, animation, freelancing, and entrepreneurship. Unlike platforms like Udemy where students buy individual courses, Skillshare operates on a Netflix-style subscription model. Members pay a monthly subscription for unlimited access to all classes, while teachers are paid from a shared Royalty Pool based on total minutes watched.

2. How Skillshare Teacher Earnings Work

Skillshare allocates 30% of its subscription revenue to a Teacher Royalty Pool each month. Your earnings are calculated dynamically:

  • Royalty Per Minute Watched: You earn royalties for every minute watched by premium subscribers in your classes.
  • Referral Bonuses: Earn $10 for every new student who signs up for a Skillshare membership using your custom referral link.

3. Popular Teaching Categories

  • 🎨 Graphic Design & Illustration: Procreate drawing, logo design, typography, and Canva masterclasses.
  • 📷 Photography & Video Production: Mobile photo editing, lighting techniques, and Premiere Pro video editing.
  • 💼 Freelancing & Entrepreneurship: Building a portfolio, managing client contracts, and productivity setups.
  • ✍️ Creative Writing & Blogging: Content creation strategies, SEO writing, and storytelling.

4. Step-by-Step Guide to Publishing Your First Class

  1. Choose a specific topic with a hands-on student project (e.g. "Design a Vintage Logo in 30 Minutes").
  2. Create a class outline breaking lessons into 2- to 5-minute video bites.
  3. Record video lessons using HD video and clean microphone audio.
  4. Upload class videos, set a cover image, and publish on Skillshare.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is publishing a class on Skillshare free?

Yes, applying to teach and hosting video classes on Skillshare is completely free.

Q: When do teachers get paid?

Payouts are processed on the 16th of every month for royalties earned during the previous calendar month.
