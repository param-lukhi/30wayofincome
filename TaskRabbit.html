What is TaskRabbit?

TaskRabbit ek platform hai jo clients aur workers (Taskers) ko connect karta hai. Clients local tasks post karte hain aur Taskers un tasks ko complete by doing money earnte hain.

  • Furniture assembly
  • Moving help
  • Cleaning
  • Delivery
  • Handyman services

How TaskRabbit Works? (Step-by-Step)

  1. TaskRabbit par free account create to proceed
  2. Profile aur skills add to proceed
  3. Hourly rate set to proceed
  4. Nearby tasks receive to proceed
  5. Client aapko book karta hai
  6. Task complete to proceed
  7. Payment direct bank me milti hai

How to Create TaskRabbit Account

  • Visit: www.taskrabbit.com
  • Click on Become a Tasker
  • Email aur phone number se signup to proceed
  • Identity verification complete to proceed
  • Skills & categories choose to proceed
  • Hourly rate set to proceed
  • Profile approval ka wait to proceed

Note: TaskRabbit sirf selected countries aur cities me available hai.
