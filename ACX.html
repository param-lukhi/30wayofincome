What is ACX?

ACX is an audiobook marketplace owned by Amazon. It connects authors, publishers, narrators, and producers to create professional audiobooks. Once an audiobook is completed, it is distributed on Audible, Amazon, and Apple Books (iTunes).

ACX allows narrators and producers to earn money either through one-time payments or long-term royalty sharing.

How ACX Works (Step-by-Step)

  • Create a free ACX account
  • Set up your narrator or producer profile
  • Upload voice samples or audio demos
  • Browse available audiobook projects
  • Audition for titles
  • Get selected by the author
  • Record, edit, and upload chapters
  • Get paid or earn royalties
