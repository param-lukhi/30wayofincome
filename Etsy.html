Etsy Seller Guide 2026: Sell Digital Products & Print on Demand

Reviewed by Editorial Team | Updated for 2026

Marketplace Niche Handmade, Vintage & Digital Goods
Listing Fee $0.20 per product listing
Transaction Fee 6.5% transaction fee per sale
Payout Methods Etsy Payments, Direct Deposit, PayPal
Earning Potential $300 – $4,000+ / month

1. What is Etsy?

Etsy is a global online marketplace specializing in unique, handmade, vintage items, and digital products. Unlike general platforms like Amazon or eBay, Etsy attracts buyers actively looking for personalized gifts, creative crafts, digital printables, and home decor items.

2. Popular Product Categories for Beginners

  • 📱 Digital Downloads & Printables: Digital planners, budget trackers, resume templates, and printable wall art (100% passive income after uploading).
  • 👕 Print-On-Demand (POD): Custom printed T-shirts, tote bags, and coffee mugs powered by integrations like Printify or Printful.
  • 💍 Handmade Jewelry & Crafting: Custom engraved items, handmade soaps, candles, and knitted accessories.
  • 📦 Craft Supplies & Vintage Items: Crafting tools, beads, patterns, and vintage apparel over 20 years old.

3. Etsy Fee Structure Breakdown

Fee Type Cost
Listing Fee $0.20 USD per listing (renews every 4 months or upon sale).
Transaction Fee 6.5% of the total order amount (including shipping price).
Payment Processing Fee Varies by country (approx. 3% + $0.25 USD).

4. Steps to Launch Your Etsy Shop

  1. Sign up at Etsy.com and select Sell on Etsy.
  2. Set shop preferences (language, currency, shop name).
  3. Create your first product listing with high-resolution photos and descriptive tags.
  4. Set up your billing and bank account for Etsy Payments deposits.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you need inventory to sell on Etsy?

No. By selling digital downloads (PDFs, templates) or print-on-demand goods, you can run an Etsy shop with zero physical inventory.

Q: How does Etsy pay sellers?

Earnings are deposited directly into your bank account via Etsy Payments according to your chosen schedule (daily, weekly, or monthly).
