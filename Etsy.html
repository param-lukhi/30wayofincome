What is Etsy?

Etsy ek e-commerce platform hai jahan sellers handmade products, digital files aur creative items sell karte hain. Yahan buyers already shopping mindset me aate hain, isliye selling comparatively easy hoti hai.

How Etsy Works? (Simple Explanation)

  • Free Etsy seller account banate ho
  • Shop create karte ho
  • Products list karte ho
  • Customer order karta hai
  • Digital file auto-deliver hoti hai / POD print hota hai
  • Payment aapke account me aata hai

How to Create Etsy Seller Account (Step-by-Step)

  • Website open karein: www.etsy.com
  • Click on Sell on Etsy
  • Email se sign up karein
  • Shop name choose karein
  • First product list karein
