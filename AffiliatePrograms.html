What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate Marketing ek commission-based business model hai jahan aap kisi company ka product ya service recommend karte ho.

Jab koi user aapke affiliate link se product kharidta hai, to aapko commission milta hai.

How Affiliate Marketing Works? (Simple Explanation)

  • Aap affiliate program join karte ho
  • Company aapko unique affiliate link deti hai
  • Aap link blog, YouTube, Instagram ya website par share karte ho
  • User link par click karke product buy karta hai
  • Aapko commission milta hai
