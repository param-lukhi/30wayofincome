What Are Affiliate Programs?

Affiliate programs are partnerships where companies pay you a commission for bringing customers to their products or services. You promote a product using a unique affiliate link, and when someone purchases through that link, you earn money.

Affiliate marketing does not require product creation, customer support, or inventory management.

How Affiliate Marketing Works

  • You join an affiliate program
  • You get a unique tracking link
  • You promote the link via website, blog, or social media
  • A visitor clicks your link
  • The visitor makes a purchase
  • You earn a commission
