1. What is PeoplePerHour?

PeoplePerHour (PPH) is a UK-based global freelancing platform connecting businesses with vetted freelance experts. Renowned for custom service packages called "Offers" (fixed-price services completed in a set timeframe) as well as traditional project proposal bidding, PPH is widely used across web development, design, digital marketing, and writing.

2. How to Win Work on PeoplePerHour

  • Get Approved: Submit a detailed application showcasing your work portfolio, social profiles, and specific skills. PPH manually reviews applications to maintain quality.
  • Post Custom Offers: Create packaged services with clear deliverables (e.g., "I will design a modern logo in 24 hours for $50").
  • Submit Targeted Proposals: Use your monthly proposal credits to bid on client job postings with custom-tailored proposals.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is PeoplePerHour free to join?

Yes, registration is free. Freelancers receive 15 free proposal credits per month.

Q: How does PeoplePerHour protect payment?

PPH uses an Escrow system where clients deposit funds prior to project commencement, releasing them upon work completion.
