What is PeoplePerHour?

PeoplePerHour ek online marketplace hai jahan businesses aur clients freelancers ko hire karte hain web development, design, writing, marketing aur bahut saari services ke liye.

Is platform par aap:

  • Hourly work kar sakte ho

How PeoplePerHour Works?

  • Free account create karo
  • Profile complete karo
  • Skills & services add karo
  • Projects par proposal bhejo
  • Client hire karta hai

  • Payment secure escrow se milti hai

How to Create PeoplePerHour Account (Step-by-Step)

  • Website open karein: www.peopleperhour.com
  • Sign Up par click karein
  • Email / Google / LinkedIn se register karein
  • Freelancer account select karein

⚠️ Note: PeoplePerHour profile manually approve karta hai, isliye profile strong banayein.
