What is Amazon Mechanical Turk?

MTurk ek crowdsourcing platform hai jahan businesses aur researchers simple tasks ke liye human intelligence ki zarurat rakhte hain. Ye tasks automation software nahi kar sakta, isliye human workers ko hire kiya jata hai.

Tasks ya HITs (Human Intelligence Tasks) me surveys, data entry, content moderation, image labeling, audio transcription, aur bahut saare chhote jobs shamil hain.

How Amazon Mechanical Turk Works? (Step-by-Step)

  1. Create a free Amazon account or login with existing account.
  2. Visit www.mturk.com
  3. Register as a Worker (worker account).
  4. Browse available HITs (tasks).
  5. Accept a HIT and complete the task as instructed.
  6. Submit the task for approval.
  7. Once approved, payment is added to your MTurk account.
  8. Transfer earnings to your bank or Amazon gift card.

How to Create Amazon Mechanical Turk Account

  • Visit www.mturk.com
  • Sign in with your Amazon account
  • Click "Get Started as a Worker"
  • Fill in personal details (name, address, tax info)
  • Verify email & phone number
  • Start browsing tasks

Tip: Complete your profile accurately for more task approvals.
