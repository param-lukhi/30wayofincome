Amazon Mechanical Turk 2026: Complete Beginner Guide

Introduction

Amazon Mechanical Turk, commonly known as MTurk, is one of the oldest and most trusted micro-task websites in the world. It allows people to earn money online by completing small digital tasks that computers cannot easily perform.

If you are a student, beginner, or someone looking for simple work-from-home income, MTurk can be a good starting point. This guide explains everything you need to know about Amazon Mechanical Turk in 2026.

What is Amazon Mechanical Turk?

Amazon Mechanical Turk is a crowdsourcing marketplace owned by Amazon. Businesses and researchers post small tasks known as HITs (Human Intelligence Tasks), and workers complete them for payment.

These tasks usually require human judgment, such as identifying images, answering surveys, or checking data accuracy.

How Amazon Mechanical Turk Works

  • Create a free MTurk worker account
  • Complete profile verification
  • Browse available HITs
  • Accept a task
  • Complete and submit the task
  • Requester reviews your work
  • Get paid after approval
