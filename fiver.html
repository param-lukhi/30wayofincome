How to Create a Fiverr Account

  • Go to fiverr.com
  • Click Join
  • Sign up using Email, Google, or Facebook
  • Choose a username
  • Complete your profile with photo, description, and skill details

How to Create a Fiverr Gig

A gig is your service listing. A successful gig includes:

  • Clear & keyword-rich title
  • Professional gig images or videos (use descriptive alt text)
  • Detailed description of your service
  • Correct category & subcategory
  • Competitive price

Example Gig Title:
I will design professional t-shirt graphics for print on demand
