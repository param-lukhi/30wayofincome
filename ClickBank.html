What is ClickBank?

ClickBank is an online affiliate marketplace that connects product creators with affiliate marketers. Affiliates promote products using special tracking links and earn commissions when a sale is made.

Unlike physical products, most ClickBank products are digital, which means instant delivery and high commission rates.
